Beef Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of ground beef
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 package (10 ounces) frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 envelop onion soup mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, cook beef until no longer pink, 3-5 minutes, breaking into crumbles.
  2. Drain the pan.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat
  5. Cover and simmer until vegetables are tender (aproximately 10-15 minutes).
